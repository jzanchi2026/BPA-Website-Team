OUR STORY

Stage Fright is a dynamic rock band celebrated for their raw energy, emotive lyrics, and captivating live performances. Since the fall of 2021, they’ve crafted a unique sound that fuses classic rock roots with modern innovation, delivering music that is both powerful and deeply personal. Their journey, shaped by a commitment to authenticity and resilience, has forged a profound connection with fans across the globe.

October 15, 2021 – Stage Fright is Born

Stage Fright was formed by three friends who shared a love of classic rock and a mutual struggle with social anxiety. Fueled by a desire to break out of their shells, Blaze Wilde, Raquel Vargas, and Dylan Prescott came together to create music that was bold, vulnerable, and unforgettable. The name Stage Fright became both a nod to their fears and a declaration of their defiance.

January 8th, 2022 – Debut Album: "Silent Ovation"

The band released their debut album, Silent Ovation, a raw and introspective exploration of fear and identity. Its lead single, “Breath that Lingers,” quickly became a fan favorite, thanks to its driving rhythm and haunting lyrics.

December 3rd, 2022 - Zona Music Festival

In 2022, Stage Fright made their festival debut at the Zona Music Festival, marking a major milestone in their journey. The band captivated the audience, proving that their music transcends fear and inspires connection. The performance was a defining moment, solidifying their place in the indie rock scene.

February 6th, 2023 – Sophomore Album: "Heartbreak Boulevard"

Inspired by their newly acquired fanbase, the members of Stage Fright quickly got to work on new music. Their second album, Heartbreak Boulevard, expanded their sound with more experimental instrumentation and layered storytelling. The lead single, “Splinters,” became a rock anthem, climbing the charts and solidifying the band’s place in modern rock.

April 28, 2023 – Austin Psych Fest

Stage Fright captivated audiences at Austin Psych Fest 2023 with a mesmerizing performance that perfectly embodied the festival's ethereal vibe. The band performed tracks from their latest album, Heartbreak Boulevard, weaving raw emotion and atmospheric soundscapes into every note. Their set left the crowd spellbound and solidified their reputation as one of the most compelling acts of the festival.

August 27th, 2023 – Breaking the Silence: The Tour

Stage Fright embarked on their first major headlining tour, Breaking the Silence, spanning 20 cities across North America. Known for their high-energy performances and deeply personal fan interactions, the tour was a sell-out success.

June 23rd, 2024 – Third Album: "Fade to Black"

Their third album, Fade to Black, embraced themes of resilience and reinvention. Critics praised its lead track, “Midnight Echos,” as one of the band’s most powerful and anthemic songs to date.

??? – The Future is Bright

As Stage Fright enters the next chapter of their journey, fans and critics alike are eager to see where they’ll go next. One thing is certain: their story is far from over.

MEET THE TEAM

BLAZE WILDE

FROM SCOTTSDALE, AZ

Main Guitarist - Social Media Coordinator

I got my first ever guitar for my 14th birthday, it was metallic and purple.

Blaze began learning the guitar in middle school through a school program that allowed students to choose an instrument. His natural talent and passion for the guitar quickly grew, and he has been playing ever since.

RAQUEL VARGAS

12/22/2002

FROM FRESNO, CA

Lead Singer - Merchandise Supervisor

One time I was singing at church. I forgot the words, so I made them up.

Raquel’s musical foundation was built during her childhood, singing regularly in her church choir. Growing up in a supportive environment, she developed her vocal skills and confidence.

DYLAN PRESCOTT

04/13/2001

FROM BOISE, ID

Secondary Instrumentalist - Creative Director

I hate parties with a passion, but I met Blaze and Raquel through one of them.

Dylan was introduced to the drums by his grandfather, who inspired his love for music. After receiving his first drum set as a child, Dylan has continued to develop his skills and pursue his passion for percussion.
